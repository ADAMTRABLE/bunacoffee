Who we are
Born from a love
of great coffee

Buna — the Ethiopian word for coffee — is at the heart of everything we do. We believe great coffee is more than a drink; it's a ritual, a connection, and a story.

We partner directly with small-scale farmers in Ethiopia's Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, and Harrar regions, ensuring every bean meets our exacting standards for quality and sustainability.

Each batch is carefully roasted in small quantities to preserve its unique flavor profile — earthy, floral, and powerfully aromatic.
